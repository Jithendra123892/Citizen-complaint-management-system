About the Portal

Bridging Citizens & Governance

A transparent, accountable mechanism to address civic grievances at the grassroots level.

The Challenge

Millions of citizens face civic issues daily — broken street lights, potholes, water shortages, garbage accumulation. Traditional complaint systems are slow and opaque, leaving citizens frustrated.

Our Solution

CitizenConnect transforms the way you interact with local governance. Report issues in minutes, track progress in real-time, and see your feedback drive actual change in your community.

The Impact

Thousands of active citizens are already making a difference. Complaints have led to faster resolutions, better public services, and more transparent governance across all wards.

Portal Features

Powerful Tools for Every Citizen

Everything you need to make your voice heard and track resolution of civic issues.

Instant Grievance Filing

Lodge complaints in minutes with our simple interface. Add photos, describe the issue, and select the category. No visits to government offices required.

Real-time Notifications

Stay informed at every step. Get SMS/email updates when your complaint is received, assigned to a department, in progress, or resolved.

Status Tracking Dashboard

View the current status of all your grievances from a single dashboard. Access complete history and resolution timelines at any time.

Automatic Department Routing

Complaints are automatically routed to the correct department — PWD, Water Board, Municipality, Electricity — based on issue type and location.

Process

How CitizenConnect Works

From grievance filing to resolution — a simple, transparent 4-step process.

1

Register & Login

Create a free account with your mobile number or Aadhaar-linked email. Takes less than 2 minutes.

2

File Grievance

Describe the issue, attach a photo, and pin your ward/location. Submit — your unique grievance ID is generated instantly.

3

Track Progress

Receive real-time updates via SMS/email as your grievance is processed by the concerned department.

4

Confirm Resolution

Confirm resolution and rate your experience. Your feedback helps improve service quality across the municipality.
