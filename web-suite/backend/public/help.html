Getting Started

Welcome to CitizenConnect! Here's how to get started with filing and tracking your complaints.

Registration

  • Visit the registration page and fill in your details
  • Provide your Aadhaar number for verification
  • Set a secure password
  • Verify your email and phone number

Login

Use your registered email or phone number along with your password to login to the portal.

Filing a Complaint

Step-by-Step Process

  1. Login to your CitizenConnect account
  2. Click on "File New Complaint" from the dashboard
  3. Select the appropriate category for your complaint
  4. The system will automatically assign the relevant department
  5. Fill in the complaint title and detailed description
  6. Provide the exact location of the issue
  7. Select the priority level (Low, Medium, High, or Critical)
  8. Upload any supporting documents or photos
  9. Submit your complaint
  10. Note down your complaint number for future reference

Tracking Complaints

You can track your complaint status in multiple ways:

  • From Dashboard: View all your complaints with current status
  • From My Complaints: Detailed list with filtering options
  • Track Page: Enter your complaint number to check status
  • Notifications: Receive updates on status changes

Status Meanings

  • Pending: Complaint received, awaiting assignment
  • In Progress: Officer is working on your complaint
  • Resolved: Complaint has been addressed
  • Closed: Complaint is closed after resolution
  • Rejected: Complaint was not accepted

Account Management

Updating Profile

Go to "My Profile" to update your personal information, contact details, and address.

Changing Password

You can change your password from the profile section. You'll need to provide your current password for security.

Deleting Account

If you wish to delete your account, go to "My Profile" and click on "Delete My Account". This action is irreversible.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to resolve a complaint? +
Resolution time varies based on the category and priority. Most complaints are resolved within 3-7 working days. Critical issues are prioritized and resolved faster.
Can I file a complaint without registering? +
No, you need to register on the portal to file complaints. Registration helps us verify your identity and provide better service.
What happens after I file a complaint? +
If your complaint is rejected, you will receive a notification with the reason. You can file a new complaint with corrected information or contact support for assistance.
Can I upload photos with my complaint? +
Yes, you can upload multiple photos and documents when filing a complaint. This helps officers better understand and address the issue.
How do I track my complaint status? +
You can track your complaint status from the "My Complaints" section in your dashboard. You'll receive notifications at every stage of the complaint resolution process.
